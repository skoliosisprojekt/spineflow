Post-Surgery? We've Got You.
Guided Training After Spinal Surgery

No Surgery

Full exercise library available. Guidance ratings based on your curve type alone.

Partial Spinal Fusion

Modified guidance ratings. Machine-based exercises prioritized. Custom post-fusion modifications.

Full Spinal Fusion

Conservative exercise selection. Heavy compounds restricted. Focus on isolation and machine exercises.

VBT (Vertebral Body Tethering)

Flexible correction preserved. Most exercises available with standard curve-type modifications.

Growing Rods / MAGEC

Adjustable rod system. Modified guidance ratings similar to partial fusion. Regular reassessment recommended.

"Hardgainer with scoliosis here. I'm building my own gym app because nothing out there understands our needs. Which exercises are recommended? Which ones should I avoid after fusion? I'm tired of guessing."

That Reddit post in r/scoliosis started it all. The overwhelming response from the community confirmed what we suspected: there was no gym app that truly understood scoliosis. People shared their surgery experiences, their fears about training, and their frustration with generic fitness apps.

SpineFlow was built with direct input from the scoliosis community. Every guidance rating, every post-surgery modification, and every exercise selection was informed by real stories from real people living with scoliosis — from teenagers with adolescent idiopathic scoliosis to adults years after spinal fusion.
