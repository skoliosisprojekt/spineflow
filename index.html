Post-Surgery? We've Got You.
Mindful Training After Spinal Surgery

No Surgery

Full exercise library available. Guidance ratings based on your curve type.

Partial Spinal Fusion

Adjusted guidance ratings. Machine-based exercises highlighted. Post-fusion considerations included.

Full Spinal Fusion

Conservative exercise guidance. Focus on isolation and machine exercises. Heavy compounds flagged for caution.

VBT (Vertebral Body Tethering)

Flexible correction preserved. Most exercises available with standard curve-type guidance.

Growing Rods / MAGEC

Adjustable rod system. Guidance similar to partial fusion. Regular reassessment encouraged.
